Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

Tooltastics is designed with privacy as a core principle. All tools and calculators run entirely in your browser. No data you enter is sent to our servers, stored, or shared with third parties.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. All calculations are performed locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your inputs never leave your device.

We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which collects anonymous, privacy-preserving usage statistics (page views, referrer, device type). This data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users. No cookies are used for analytics.

Cookies

Tooltastics uses no tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set essential cookies for security and performance purposes (e.g., load balancing). These do not track your activity.

Third-Party Services

Tooltastics is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare's privacy policy governs the underlying infrastructure. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Data Retention

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to retain. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Cloudflare for 30 days.

Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions, contact us at hello@tooltastics.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
