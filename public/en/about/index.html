About Tooltastics

Free online tools and calculators — accurate, private, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Tooltastics was created with a simple mission: provide high-quality, accurate online calculators and tools that are completely free, private, and accessible to everyone. We believe that essential calculation tools should never require registration, cost money, or compromise your privacy.

Every tool on Tooltastics runs entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device. No signup, no tracking, no hidden fees — just reliable calculations when you need them.

Our Values

Free Forever

Every tool is completely free with no tiers, limits, or hidden charges.

Privacy First

All calculations run locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

Accuracy

We use verified, industry-standard formulas and regularly update our tools.

Accessibility

Tools work on all devices and are designed for everyone, everywhere.
